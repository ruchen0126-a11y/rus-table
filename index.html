The Story

A Story Told
in Flavor

Where Every Meal
Tells a Story

Food has always been my way of understanding the world. From late-night ramen counters in Tokyo to farmhouse kitchens in Tuscany, I've spent years collecting flavors, techniques, and the stories woven between them.

I believe the table is the most honest place on earth — no pretense, just people and conversation. I host intimate dinners for small groups of friends and curious strangers, and I believe every meal shared is a small act of generosity.
