Food has always been how I understand the world. From night markets in Chengdu to hole-in-the-wall ramen shops in Tokyo, I have spent years chasing the meals that stay with you long after the last bite.

Ru's Table is an extension of that curiosity — a place where honest ingredients, careful technique, and genuine conversation come together in one evening.

Every menu is written around what is freshest at the market and what stories feel worth telling that night.
